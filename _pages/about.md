I am a final-year undergraduate student majoring in **Automation** at the [Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen](https://www.hitsz.edu.cn/index.html). During my undergraduate studies, I have had the privilege of working as a **Research Assistant** at the [Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School](https://www.sigs.tsinghua.edu.cn/), under the supervision of Prof. [Xueqian Wang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=h9dN_ykAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). Additionally, I conducted research at the [National University of Singapore](https://www.nus.edu.sg/) under the guidance of Prof. [Lin Shao](https://linsats.github.io/). I also gained practical industry experience as an **Intern** at [Dobot Robotics](https://www.dobot-robots.com/). 

My research interests lie primarily in **robotics** and **embodied AI**, where I aim to contribute to advancing intelligent systems and their real-world applications.
